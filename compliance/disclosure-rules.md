# Disclosure Rules

## When disclosure is required

Use disclosure language on:

- affiliate/referral content
- public partner pages
- campaign kits
- funding readiness resources
- partner onboarding emails
- lead submission confirmations
- generated GPT partner messages

## Required disclosure

> Funding options may vary. No approval, funding, rates, terms, timelines, lender review, commissions, income, or specific business outcome is guaranteed.

## Affiliate/referral disclosure

When partners promote or refer:

> This may be a referral or affiliate relationship. Any partner compensation is subject to program terms and completed review requirements.

## Do not say

- “Guaranteed approval”
- “Everyone qualifies”
- “Get approved instantly”
- “Guaranteed commissions”
- “Earn guaranteed income”
- “No documents needed”
