# Safe Language Library

## Safe phrases

- “received for review”
- “manual review required”
- “funding options may vary”
- “readiness-based review”
- “business funding options”
- “documentation requirements vary”
- “eligibility depends on business profile and provider criteria”
- “partner access is reviewed before activation”
- “campaign copy should be reviewed before public use”

## Safer rewrites

| Risky phrase | Safer rewrite |
|---|---|
| guaranteed approval | options may be reviewed |
| everyone qualifies | eligibility and options vary |
| instant funding | fast review may be available |
| risk-free | review terms and tradeoffs |
| no documents needed | documentation requirements vary |
| guaranteed commission | commission eligibility depends on program terms |
| earn guaranteed income | partner opportunities depend on approved activity and program rules |

## Baseline disclosure

No approval, funding, rates, terms, timelines, lender review, commissions, income, or specific business outcome is guaranteed.
