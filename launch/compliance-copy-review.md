# Compliance Copy Review

This review applies to partner-facing copy, lead submission pages, dashboard copy, GPT responses, campaign kits, admin notes, and any operator-written follow-up.

## Core rule

Use readiness-based, operational, review-based language. Do not make funding, approval, lender, commission, income, speed, or eligibility guarantees.

## Prohibited language

| Prohibited phrase/type | Why it fails | Safer direction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| “Guaranteed approval” | False/fake certainty. | “May be reviewed for possible options.” |
| “Guaranteed funding” | False/fake certainty. | “Funding options may vary.” |
| “Everyone qualifies” | Misleading eligibility claim. | “Eligibility depends on business profile and provider criteria.” |
| “Instant approval” | Implies underwriting outcome. | “Fast review workflow.” |
| “No risk” | Misleading financial claim. | “Review terms carefully before choosing an option.” |
| “Guaranteed commissions” | Misleading partner income claim. | “Commission eligibility depends on program rules and closed/funded outcomes.” |
| “We repair credit” | Credit repair positioning. | “Business credit readiness education.” |
| “Lender will approve” | Fake lender certainty. | “A provider may request more information.” |
| “Act now or lose funding” | Deceptive urgency. | “Timelines vary by product and provider.” |
| Invented testimonials | Fabricated social proof. | Use real approved testimonials only, or omit. |

## Safe language bank

Use these as defaults:

```text
Funding options may vary.
Submitting information does not guarantee approval, funding, rates, terms, timelines, lender review, commissions, income, or any specific business outcome.
Only submit information you have permission to share.
This system supports intake review and operational routing; it does not make underwriting decisions.
This recommendation is for internal review and should be verified before partner-facing use.
```

## Admin notes review

Admin notes should be concise and operational:

Good:

```text
Manual review required because partner claims paid lead resale and high monthly volume without source details.
```

Bad:

```text
This partner will definitely make us money and their leads should be approved fast.
```
