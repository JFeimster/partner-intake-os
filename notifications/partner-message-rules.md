# Partner Message Rules

## Partner-facing messages are guarded

Partner-facing notifications should be optional and must pass safe-language checks.

Allowed message types:

- submission received for review
- more information requested
- onboarding next step
- resource available
- campaign kit ready

Not allowed:

- approved for funding
- guaranteed approval
- guaranteed commission
- lender reviewed your lead
- instant funding
- everyone qualifies

## Required footer

Partner-facing messages should include:

> Funding options may vary. No approval, funding, rates, terms, timelines, lender review, commissions, income, or specific business outcome is guaranteed.
