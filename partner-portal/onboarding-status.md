# Partner Portal Onboarding Status

## Status values

- `not_started`
- `in_progress`
- `needs_info`
- `ready_for_review`
- `approved_for_onboarding`
- `active`
- `paused`
- `archived`

## Checklist concepts

Safe checklist items:

- complete profile
- confirm audience
- review compliant promo rules
- choose first campaign
- create first tracking link
- submit first lead for review
- schedule onboarding call

Do not include borrower-specific documents, sensitive lead data, or payout terms in local checklist storage.

## Status language

Use review-safe language:

- "received for review"
- "manual review required"
- "ready for onboarding"
- "funding options may vary"

Do not use:

- "approved for funding"
- "guaranteed commission"
- "instant approval"
- "everyone qualifies"
