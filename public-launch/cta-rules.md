# CTA Rules

## Approved CTA language

- Apply to become a partner
- Request partner access
- Start partner review
- Submit partner interest
- Check partner fit
- Request strategic partner review

## Use carefully

- Submit a client for review
- Check funding readiness
- Explore funding options

## Forbidden CTA language

- Get approved instantly
- Everyone qualifies
- Start earning guaranteed commissions
- Guaranteed funding
- No documents needed
- Risk-free funding
- Guaranteed partner income

## CTA routing

Public CTAs should point to the partner intake form, not admin routes or internal APIs.
