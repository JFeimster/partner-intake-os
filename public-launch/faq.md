# FAQ

## Is partner access guaranteed?

No. Partner requests are reviewed. Access, onboarding path, and available resources may vary.

## Do partners earn guaranteed commissions?

No. Any compensation depends on the partner agreement, program rules, completed review requirements, and applicable terms.

## Can I submit leads immediately?

Some partners may receive lead submission access after review. Others may start with an education-first or referral-only path.

## Is this only for brokers?

No. It supports brokers, referral partners, affiliates, CPAs/bookkeepers, attorneys, veteran/community connectors, fintech/vendor partners, and strategic partners.

## Does this guarantee business funding?

No. Funding options may vary. No approval, funding, rates, terms, timelines, lender review, or specific business outcome is guaranteed.

## Where do I apply?

Use the public intake flow linked from the launch pages.
