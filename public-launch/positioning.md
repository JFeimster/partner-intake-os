# Public Launch Positioning

## One-liner

Partner Intake OS turns partner interest into structured, review-ready operating intelligence.

## Public positioning

Built for brokers, referral partners, affiliates, CPAs/bookkeepers, attorneys, veteran/community connectors, fintech/vendor partners, and strategic partners who want a cleaner way to route business funding conversations.

## Core pain

Most partner intake is chaos: DMs, forms, calls, spreadsheets, half-baked referrals, and “trust me bro” lead claims. Partner Intake OS puts structure around who the partner is, what they serve, how they should onboard, and what the next action should be.

## Promise

A clearer partner review process. Not guaranteed access, funding, approval, lender review, commissions, income, or outcomes.

## Tone

Direct, practical, conversion-focused, and compliance-clean.
