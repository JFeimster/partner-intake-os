What Partner Intake OS actually does

From Raw Intake to Revenue-Ready Intelligence

Not magic. Not a cute chatbot. A structured intake operator that turns partner noise into reviewable, routable, CRM-ready records.

PT

Classifies Partner Type

Identifies whether the intake looks like a broker, ISO, referral partner, CPA/bookkeeper, attorney, business broker, community connector, affiliate, vendor, strategic partner, or not-fit partner.

QS

Scores Partner Quality

Evaluates audience access, trust, deal-flow potential, activation speed, technical readiness, strategic value, relationship quality, and compliance risk.

T1

Recommends Partner Tier

Assigns practical tiering such as Tier 1 strategic/channel partner, Tier 2 active referral partner, Tier 3 new/unproven partner, Tier 4 nurture/watchlist, or reject/manual review.

OP

Creates Onboarding Path

Recommends fast-track onboarding, standard affiliate onboarding, referral-only path, education-first path, strategic review, nurture/watchlist, or risk review.

CRM

Produces CRM-Ready Fields

Outputs structured partner notes, tags, status, risk level, next action, resources, campaign recommendations, and dashboard-ready records.

KIT

Recommends Resources and Campaigns

Suggests the right resource pack, lead magnet, campaign angle, CTA, channel mix, and activation path based on partner type and audience.
