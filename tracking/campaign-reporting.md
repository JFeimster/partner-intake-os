# Campaign Reporting

## Summary metrics

- links created
- clicks
- lead starts
- lead submissions
- manual reviews
- resource opens
- campaign kit views
- conversion from click to lead submitted

## Reporting constraints

Campaign reports should show operational performance, not earnings guarantees.

Allowed phrasing:

- "This campaign generated 12 submitted leads for review."
- "Click-to-submission rate was 8%."

Not allowed:

- "This campaign should earn $X."
- "This campaign guarantees approvals."
- "This partner is guaranteed to convert."

## Reporting windows

- 7 days
- 30 days
- 90 days
- custom server-side window
